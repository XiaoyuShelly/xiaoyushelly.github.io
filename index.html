About

Hi! I'm Xiaoyu (Shelly), a PhD student in the Computer Science and Information Technology at La Trobe University. My research sits at the intersection of Virtual Reality, embodied interaction, and interaction design.

I am currently working on exploring natural and embodied input and output methods for VR. For example, I investigate how natural body gestures can be used to control non-walking locomotion in VR, and how natural vibrotactile interactions can assist with target selection.

Prior to this, my research mainly focused on data visualization and interaction design , particularly in the areas of emotion awareness and stress relief.
